NERC CIP-004-6 — Personnel & Training
Requirement R4 — Access Management Program

R4.1. The responsible entity shall implement a documented access-management program that authorizes electronic access and unescorted physical access to BES Cyber Systems based on need, as determined by the responsible entity.

R4.2. The entity shall verify at least once each calendar quarter that individuals with active electronic or physical access have authorization records.

R4.3. For provisioned access, the entity shall ensure that access privileges are correct and are those that the entity determines are necessary.
