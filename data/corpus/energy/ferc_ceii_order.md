FERC Order No. 833 (excerpt) — Critical Energy/Electric Infrastructure Information (CEII)

CEII means information about proposed or existing critical electric infrastructure that is designated as critical by the Commission, is not generally available, and could be useful to a person planning an attack on critical infrastructure.

Disclosure of CEII is restricted. A person seeking CEII must demonstrate a need to know and execute a non-disclosure agreement before access is granted. Voluntary submitters of CEII may request CEII treatment, and the Commission will protect such information from public release.
