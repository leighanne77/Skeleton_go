State Public Utility Commission — Service Quality Rule 14-302 (synthetic)
Retail Electric Service — Reliability and Reporting

Each electric distribution utility shall report the System Average Interruption Duration Index (SAIDI) and the System Average Interruption Frequency Index (SAIFI) to the Commission annually by April 1.

A utility whose SAIDI exceeds the Commission-established baseline by more than 20 percent in a reporting year shall file a corrective action plan within 60 days. Major event days may be excluded from the indices consistent with IEEE Standard 1366.
