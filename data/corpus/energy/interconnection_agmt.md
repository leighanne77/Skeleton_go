Large Generator Interconnection Agreement (LGIA) — Summary Terms (synthetic)
Parties: Northwind Energy (Transmission Provider) and Cedar Ridge Solar LLC (Interconnection Customer).

The Interconnection Customer shall be responsible for the cost of Network Upgrades identified in the facilities study, estimated at $4.2 million, payable per the milestone schedule in Appendix C.

The point of interconnection is the high side of the Customer's step-up transformer at the Cedar Ridge collector substation. Commercial operation is targeted for 2027-06-01, subject to completion of the required Network Upgrades.
