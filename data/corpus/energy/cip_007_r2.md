NERC CIP-007-6 — System Security Management
Requirement R2 — Security Patch Management

R2.1. A responsible entity shall implement a documented patch-management process that includes the identification of a source or sources that the entity tracks for the release of cyber security patches for applicable Cyber Assets.

R2.2. At least once every 35 calendar days, evaluate security patches for applicability that have been released since the last evaluation from the source or sources identified in Part 2.1.

R2.3. For applicable patches identified in Part 2.2, within 35 calendar days of completing the evaluation, apply the applicable patches, or create a dated mitigation plan, or revise an existing mitigation plan.

Measure: Evidence may include patch-evaluation records, dated mitigation plans, and records of patch application for each applicable Cyber Asset.
