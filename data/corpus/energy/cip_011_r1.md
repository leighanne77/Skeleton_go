NERC CIP-011-2 — Information Protection
Requirement R1 — Information Protection Program

R1.1. The responsible entity shall have a documented information protection program that includes one or more methods to identify BES Cyber System Information (BCSI).

R1.2. The program shall include one or more methods to protect and securely handle BCSI, including storage, transit, and use.

BCSI is information about a BES Cyber System that could be used to gain unauthorized access to, or pose a security threat to, the BES Cyber System. Designated storage locations for BCSI must be controlled, and access must be limited to personnel with a documented need.
